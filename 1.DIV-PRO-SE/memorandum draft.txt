A. Preservation Credits (23 Pa.C.S. Â§ 3502(a)(6))

Pennsylvania law explicitly mandates that courts consider the "contribution or dissipation of each party in the acquisition, preservation, depreciation or appreciation of the marital property" when equitably distributing assets (23 Pa.C.S.
